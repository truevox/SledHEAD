The Mountain Awaits...

From childhood memories on Grandpa’s hill, where the snow was always whiter above and dirtier below, a dream was born. A dream of conquering the ultimate slope, of becoming a legend. Welcome to the exhilarating world of SledHEAD!

Get ready to carve your legacy into the frosty peaks of Debumont and beyond! SledHEAD is an upcoming Trick & Time Trial Racer Adventure. You are an aspiring sledding champion and content creator, destined to master an enormous, wild mountain.

What is SledHEAD?

It's more than just sledding; it's an epic adventure! You've purchased an entire procedurally generated mountain to train and broadcast your extreme sled runs. Optimize your descents for breathtaking speed and dazzling tricks, manage your stamina on the challenging climbs, and watch your fanbase grow with every daring feat!

Your journey begins on Debumont, a welcoming peak famed for its rolling groomers and cozy village charm. Learn the ropes, pay off your initial debts, and prepare to unlock a universe of new, challenging mountains. Each peak boasts unique environments and mechanics – from the treacherous, icy paths of the Garbage Dump Glacier, to the sun-scorched dunes of Pyramid Peak, and even into the neon-drenched, digital depths of Bitstream Bluffs!

But there's so much more to discover! The mountain is alive with opportunities: engage in thrilling minigames like wildlife photography, treasure hunting through digging and panning, high-flying kite adventures, meticulous beekeeping, exciting RC sledding, and even sweet sap sugaring. You'll meet a cast of quirky and helpful NPCs like Jake the old sledboarding pro, Bearwhisperer Carrie, the liaison to the sled-racing bears, the ever-hospitable Aria at the Vermont Homestyle Hotel, Minnie with her treasure-finding tools, and Pete the Beekeeper, who knows the secrets of the forest. They'll offer quests, upgrades, and bring the world of SledHEAD to vibrant life!
